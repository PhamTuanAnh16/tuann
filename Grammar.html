PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronouns are classified as followsL:
Personal pronouns:It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.
Interrogative pronouns:It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
Indefinite pronouns:It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
Relative pronouns:It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or experssoion preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
Bemonstrative pronouns:A noun or pronoun is specifed and identifed by it
Reflexive pronouns:It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
